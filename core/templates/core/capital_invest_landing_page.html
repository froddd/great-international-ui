Why choose the UK

As the world’s fifth largest economy, the UK’s global reach, regulatory clarity and rich talent pool help large capital projects realise their full potential.

Regulatory certainty

The UK government actively helps foreign investors understand domestic policy and regulations. See how we help below.

A global reach

The UK is a leading financial and technology market, playing host to some of the world’s biggest companies.

An innovation hub

The UK ranks top 5 on the G20 Global Innovation Index, with talent from the world's top universities.

Large scale capital projects

How we help

At the Department for International Trade, we work with overseas investors looking to allocate capital in the UK. As a government department dedicated to making foreign investment easy, we can:

  • Give guidance on UK regulations and policy

  • Work with UK organisations to ensure their offer is investable

  • Align international investors with credible projects pipeline

  • Help international investors navigate their investment journey

Contact our investment team

Our team are located at high commissions, embassies and consulates around the world.
